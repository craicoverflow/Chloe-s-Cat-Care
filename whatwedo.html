What We Do

Our main purpose is for people to find family in felines. We want people to bring unfortunate cats into their homes. We give you the necessary information to look after cats and encourage you to contact us if you require additional information about a certain topic. You can also donate to the cause so we can provide money to the people who are struggling to afford to look after their pets properly. We hold fundraisers and welcome any volunteers to take part in the events. You can buy cat related items and products needed for feline care in our shop, where all proceeds go into funding for veterinary practices.
