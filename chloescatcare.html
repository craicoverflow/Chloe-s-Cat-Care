What is Chloe's Cat Care?

We are an organisation that rescues street cats and give them a new home. We also provide funds to homeless or poor people to cover the price of vaccinations, flea and worming treatments, eye and ear infections and surgical procedures by fundraising. We'd appreciate volunteers!

On our website you can learn about the proper care and treatment you should give to your feline pet.

In our shop, you can buy cat plushies, t-shirts, stationery, cat toys, cat food and lots more!
